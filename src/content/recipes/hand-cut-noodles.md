---
title: "Hand-Cut Egg-Free Noodles"
description: "Fresh noodles from a simple flour, semolina, and water dough — no egg, no dairy, no pasta machine. Perfect under the stir-fry glaze."
category: "Basics"
serves: "4"
prepTime: "45 min (incl. rest)"
cookTime: "3–4 min"
tags: ["Egg free", "Dairy free", "Vegan", "Pairs with stir-fry"]
source: "https://biancazapatka.com/en/homemade-vegan-pasta/"
ingredients:
  - "150 g plain flour (or spelt flour), plus extra for dusting"
  - "150 g fine semolina flour"
  - "½ tsp fine salt"
  - "150 ml water"
  - "2 tsp olive oil"
---

1. **Make the dough** — Mix the flour, semolina, and salt in a bowl and make a well in the centre. Pour in the water and olive oil and bring it together into a shaggy dough.
2. **Knead** — Turn out onto the worktop and knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth and soft. If it feels sticky, work in a little more flour; if dry and crumbly, wet your hands and keep kneading.
3. **Rest** — Shape into a ball, wrap or cover, and rest in the fridge for 30 minutes so the gluten relaxes and the semolina hydrates.
4. **Roll and cut** — Divide the dough in two. On a well-floured surface, roll one half out thin, dusting with flour as you go. Loosely roll the sheet up into a flat cigar and cut across into strips with a sharp knife — thin for noodles, wider for ribbons. Unfurl and toss into loose, floured bundles. Repeat with the other half.
5. **Cook** — Drop into a large pan of boiling salted water for 3–4 minutes until tender but still with bite. Drain and toss straight into your sauce — they take the stir-fry glaze brilliantly.

> **Pro-tip:** Fresh noodles keep cooking in a hot sauce, so pull them from the water slightly early. Uncooked bundles freeze well on a floured tray — cook from frozen, adding a minute.
