---
title: "Berry & Oat Snack Bars"
description: "Soft, squidgy bars from just four ingredients — oats, banana, berries, and chia. No added sugar, and free of dairy, egg, gluten, and nuts."
category: "Toddler Snacks"
serves: "Makes 16"
prepTime: "5 min"
cookTime: "18 min"
tags: ["Dairy free", "Egg free", "Nut free", "No added sugar", "Freezes well"]
source: "https://www.myfussyeater.com/berry-oat-baby-weaning-bars/"
ingredients:
  - "200 g frozen berries"
  - "1 ripe banana, mashed"
  - "1 tbsp chia seeds"
  - "150 g oats (certified gluten free if needed)"
---

1. **Prep** — Preheat the oven to 180°C and line a baking dish with parchment paper.
2. **Soften the berries** — Cook the frozen berries in a saucepan with 2 tablespoons of water for a few minutes until softened. Mash them with a potato masher, then take off the heat.
3. **Mix** — Stir in the mashed banana and chia seeds, then add the oats and mix thoroughly.
4. **Bake** — Pour the mixture into the lined dish, press it down evenly, and bake at 180°C for 18 minutes.
5. **Cool and cut** — Cool briefly, then cut into 16 squares. Serve cold, at room temperature, or warmed briefly.

> **Pro-tip:** Once cooled, the bars keep in the fridge for up to 4 days. To freeze, wrap bars individually in parchment so they don't stick together — they defrost at room temperature in a couple of hours.
